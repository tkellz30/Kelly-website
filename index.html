    Process

    How It Works

    Simple, no-hassle process — no ticket queues, no hold music, no upsells.

    01

    Reach Out

    ▼

    Fill out the contact form below or send me a direct message. Just tell me what's going on — what device, what problem, how long it's been happening. The more detail you give me, the faster I can help. No tech speak required.

    02

    Quick Consult

    ▼

    I'll follow up — usually within a few hours — to make sure I understand what you need and pick the right service. We'll figure out if remote screen share works for your situation or if you'd prefer I come to you in person (Pensacola area only). No commitment yet.

    03

    We Fix It

    ▼

    We schedule a session and get to work. I walk you through everything in plain English as we go — you'll know exactly what I'm doing and why. Most sessions take 30–60 minutes. I won't leave until the problem is actually solved, not just patched over.

    04

    You're Covered

    ▼

    After the session you'll have a more secure, better-running setup and actually understand what changed and why. If you want ongoing protection, the Monthly Maintenance plan keeps me in your corner — monthly check-ins, scam reviews, and priority response whenever something pops up.
